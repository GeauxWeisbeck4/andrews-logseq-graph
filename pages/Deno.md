- # Deno Notes Home

	  ([/ˈdiːnoʊ/](https://ipa-reader.com/?text=%CB%88di%CB%90no%CA%8A), pronounced
	  `dee-no`) is an
	  [open source](https://github.com/denoland/deno/blob/main/LICENSE.md) JavaScript,

	  [Rust](https://www.rust-lang.org/), and [Tokio](https://tokio.rs/).
